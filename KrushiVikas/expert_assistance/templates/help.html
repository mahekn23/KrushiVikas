DO YOU HAVE QUESTIONS?


WE HAVE ANSWERS! Well, most of the times...


Below you will find answers to the most common questions you may have on crop prediction, disease detection of leaves, tool renting and more. We may have solutions to problems as simple as logging in as well. So, please take a look and if you cannot find what you are looking for, please feel free to contact us anytime. HAPPY FARMING!





SIGN UP:

Our sign up requires some basic details like your name, a valid email id and password. Once you provide us with those details and verify your email, we wil have an account ready for you to use all the features available.



EMAIL VERIFICATION:

Once you sign up for KrushiVikas, you will recieve an email having an One Time Password (OTP) for email verification. On the application, you will be directed to the email verification page asking for that OTP. Once your email is verified, you can log into the website.



LOG IN:

For logging into the website, we will need your registered email id and password for authentication purposes. If you are asked to verify your email first while logging in, you need to visit the email verification page and enter the OTP provided to you via email at the time of Sign Up.


CROP PREDICTION:

For knowing which crop to grow based on the conditions of weather and soil on your field, you need to provide some details like, sodium content of soil, phosphorus content of soil, potassium content of soil, air temperature, humidity of soil, pH of soil and rainfall in mm.

Once you provide valid values, you will get the most suitable crop to be grown along with the some information regarding the same.



PLANT DISEASE DETECTION:

Here you will be required to click a clear picture of a leaf of the plant you think is infected and upload it. We will let you know the disease, prevention or cure regarding the same.



RENTING TOOLS:

A detailed process of tool renting is provided HERE.



WEATHER:

All we need is a city name and you will get the weather details of that region at just a click of the button.



NEWS:

This feature provides you with the top ten headlines of the day from the most renowned NEWs portals of India.
